RAWLE NIGHT LONG

Based in Washington DC, Rawle Becerril AKA Rawle Night Long is a DJ and tastemaker in the forefront of dance music’s emerging new wave. Breaking into the culture in 2015, his versatility and quirky sensibilities as a curator earned him cult like status overnight.


Over the span of his career, he has shared bills with varied acts like Justin Martin, Will Clarke, Solardo, Disclosure, Waze & Odyssey, Yellow Claw, Adventure Club, and NGHTMRE. In 2016, he was featured on Sirius XM Electric Area in their Guest Room mix series, and returned in 2017 with a mix showcasing his love for Baltimore and Jersey club.


Rawle’s contagious energy is put on display every time he takes the stage. He dances like no one is watching and hopes you do to.
